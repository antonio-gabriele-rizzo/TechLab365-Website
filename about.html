About Me


Welcome

My name is Antonio Gabriele Rizzo. I am an IT Support professional currently specialising in Microsoft 365, Microsoft Entra ID, Exchange Online and Microsoft Intune.


My Journey

My passion for technology began during childhood through electronics and computers. In 1993 I wanted to study Information Technology, but at the time personal computers were still considered a niche technology and I was encouraged to follow a different professional path. Although my career led me first into electronics and later into healthcare, my fascination with technology never disappeared. Throughout my NHS career I became increasingly interested in the systems supporting healthcare rather than simply using them. Eventually I decided to stop wondering "what if?" and invest in building the IT career I had originally envisioned.


Why TechLab365?

TechLab365 is my personal IT laboratory where I build, test and document Microsoft technologies using a live Microsoft 365 environment. Rather than simply studying theory, I believe the best way to learn is by designing real solutions and documenting every stage of the process.
